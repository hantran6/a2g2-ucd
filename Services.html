Accessibility Statement

Your Accessibility Matters to Us

War Remnants Museum has taken careful measures to ensure a wonderful user experience, regardless of the assistive technology being used to access this site or the particular abilities of those seeking access to this site. This is because we want to provide a fully accessible and optimized user experience for all site visitors.

Immersive Reader 

On the War Remnants Museum website, we work closely with Google to add a feature called Immersive Reader. This feature triggers the bot to read the content on the website aloud and guide them to click on the next button to achieve their goals. With this support, we guarantee that users with disabilities will not be restricted from using our website.
