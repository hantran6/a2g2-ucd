About Us

Hello, and thank you for visiting the War Remnants Museum's official website!

People who visit will be able to find historical evidence of French and American invasions spanning more than a century. 

This location is responsible for the storage of more than 20,000 documents, artefacts, and films, of which more than 1,500 documents, artefacts, and films have been displayed in nine different display categories. Over 15 million people from inside and outside the country have visited the Museum in its 35 years of operation.
